About Me

⇣

Background

After moving around the Midwest my whole childhood, my family settled in Rochester, Minnesota. I always was a "well-rounded" kid. Participating in activities like ballet, volleyball, and karate as a kid gave me great experiences, but eventually, my love for music and creative expression became my focus. I was gifted a camcorder on my 9th birthday, and I began making videos with my stuffed animals and dolls. With only a camcorder and Windows Movie Maker, I was making everything from music videos and stop-motions with my dolls, to fake TV shows with my friends.

At age 10, I started taking flute lessons and eventually joined my middle school band. I continued playing my instrument in high school, and also joined the marching band, winter drumline, and jazz band. Joining my high school marching band ignited my deep passion for the marching arts. Being selected as a drum major for our band helped me develop my confidence and leadership skills. I marched drum corps in the summers during my first two years of college, while also participating in my college marching band each fall. I'm forever grateful for the opportunity to have learned from UW-Eau Claire's renowned music faculty while continuing to explore my passion for digital media.
